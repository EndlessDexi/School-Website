Motionless In White

Motionless in White is an alternative band that likes to have dark themed items in their songs and videos.

Suicide Silence

Suicide Silence is a screamo band that with a lot of their songs they have a good message in the song. For instance, the song You Only Live Once, it puts the meaning you only get one chance so live your life to the fullest. The lead singer of the band is Mitch Lucker but very sadly he died in a motorcycle accident back in 2012.
